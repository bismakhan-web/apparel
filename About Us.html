About Us

Apparel is emerging as a front runner in the apparel market and rising to the ranks of customer favorite for its premium quality fabric, originality of design, and a competitive price point. In a market inundated with brands that over commit and under deliver, Apparel is the exception that offers an extensive range of highly popular women’s wear, kids, accessories and makeup with an authenticity that stays true to the company’s ethos. The philosophy is simple; an affordable luxe product for women in different segments of society. It's a brand which is quintessentially a celebration of women, a sincere endeavor to keep a finger on the buyer’s pulse by researching latest trends, distinct color palettes, and ensuring only breathable fabrics yielded from the highest quality of polyester, cotton and blended yarn for domestic and international markets.
After serving its connoisseurs in Pakistan with its highly coveted clothing and accessories collections, Apparel is now being made available to its global fan base by launching international shipping. The collections will be made available online and only a click away for patrons living abroad who can simply visit the Apparel website, sign up and place an order, for it to be shipped anywhere in the world. locally, the widely loved brand has rapidly become a household name by churning out consistently sought after collections and has expanded to outlets in major cities of the country. The key component to its success lies in paying close attention to what women want which is maintaining the best quality of fabric combined with exquisite designs that are versatile for every season and occasion.
